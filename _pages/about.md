I am a PhD student at Imperial College London, supervised by [Professor Danilo Mandic](https://profiles.imperial.ac.uk/d.mandic). My research interests include convolutional neural networks, computer vision, large language models, mobile edge computing, signal processing, and image processing. Beyond research, I enjoy badminton, archery, photography, FPS games, and anime — feel free to reach out!

Previously, I received my B.Eng. from the School of Software, Beihang University, and my M.Phil. from The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, supervised by [Prof. Shuguang Cui](https://sse.cuhk.edu.cn/en/faculty/cuishuguang).

我是帝国理工学院（Imperial College London）在读博士生，导师为 [Professor Danilo Mandic](https://profiles.imperial.ac.uk/d.mandic)。我的研究方向包括卷积神经网络、计算机视觉、大语言模型、移动边缘计算、信号处理与图像处理。在学术之外，我也热爱生活——欢迎找我聊聊羽毛球、射箭、摄影、FPS 游戏或二次元。

此前，我于香港中文大学（深圳）取得哲学硕士学位，导师为[崔曙光教授](https://sse.cuhk.edu.cn/en/faculty/cuishuguang)，并于北京航空航天大学软件学院取得工学学士学位。
